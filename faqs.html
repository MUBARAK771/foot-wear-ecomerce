Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine my correct size? +
We recommend checking our detailed size guide available on every product page. Most of our shoes run true to size, but for half sizes, we generally suggest sizing up.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day "no questions asked" return policy. Shoes must be in their original condition and packaging. Return shipping is free for all domestic orders.
Are your materials sustainable? +
Yes, sustainability is at our core. We use recycled PET, FSC-certified eucalyptus tree fiber, and ZQ-certified merino wool. Our goal is to be carbon neutral by 2028.
Can I order in bulk? +
For corporate gifting or large team orders, please contact our concierge team directly via the contact page for specialized pricing and customized options.
How should I clean my FOOT WEARS? +
Most of our knit models are machine washable on a delicate cycle. We recommend removing the insoles and laces and using a mesh bag. Air dry only—never tumble dry.
